Specialization Rationale

Marketing Management

Focuses on consumer psychology, brand positioning, and digital marketing, preparing you for roles in brand management and market research.

Business Management

Broad understanding of business, covering finance, marketing, and leadership, suited for managers and entrepreneurs.

Finance

For those interested in financial analysis and wealth management, leading to careers in investment banking and corporate finance.

FinTech

Combines finance and technology, focusing on innovations like blockchain and AI-driven financial solutions.

HR and Analytics

Focuses on talent management and people analytics, preparing you for roles in HR management and organizational consulting.

Operations Management

Covers efficiency, productivity, and supply chain management, suited for careers in logistics and consulting.

Data Science

For those passionate about data analytics and machine learning, offering roles in predictive analytics and business intelligence.

International Finance

Focuses on global markets and cross-border transactions, leading to careers in multinational corporations and global banks.
