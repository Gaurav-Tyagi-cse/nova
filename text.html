MCA Course Information

About the Course

The Master of Computer Applications (MCA) is a professional postgraduate program that focuses on advanced theoretical and practical knowledge in computer science and application development. Designed for students seeking careers in the IT industry, the course combines foundational concepts with modern technology trends.

About Approval

MCA programs are approved by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). Institutions offering MCA programs undergo rigorous evaluations to ensure quality education.

Eligibility

  • Bachelor's degree with at least 50% marks (45% for reserved categories).
  • Mathematics as a subject in 10+2 or graduation.
  • Valid score in MCA entrance exams such as NIMCET or state-level tests.
